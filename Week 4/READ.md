## Week 4: Insight Generation, Reporting, and Final Submission

This week focuses on analyzing the Power BI dashboard to extract meaningful business insights and provide actionable recommendations.

---

### Key Activities:

* Interpreted dashboard visualizations to understand sales patterns
* Extracted key findings from sales, product, and city performance
* Developed actionable business recommendations
* Prepared a comprehensive final report documenting insights

---

### Key Insights:

* Sales remain consistent across weekdays, indicating stable customer demand
* Monthly sales show fluctuations with stronger performance during mid-year
* Electronics category contributes the highest revenue
* Kolkata is the top-performing city
* Online and Store channels contribute significantly to total sales

---

### Recommendations:

* Focus marketing efforts during peak-performing months
* Promote high-performing products like Electronics
* Strengthen operations in top-performing cities
* Improve strategies for lower-performing categories
* Optimize performance across key sales channels

---

### Deliverables:

* Final Report (PDF)
* Dashboard Screenshots
* Power BI Dashboard File

---

### Outcome:

The project provides actionable insights into retail sales performance and supports data-driven business decision-making.
